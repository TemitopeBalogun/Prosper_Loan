by Temitope Balogun¶

Investigation Overview¶

Prosper is a US based lending marketplace, with over 21 billion dollars in funded loans. Prosper generates revenue by collecting a one-time fee on funded loans from borrowers and assessing an annual loan servicing fee to investors. Borrowers can apply online which makes the process seamless.

Dataset Overview¶

The detailed dataset consists of 113,937 rows and 81 colums.

Relationship between Prosper score and Loan amount¶

Borrowers that have high prosper score are qualified to apply for high amounts of loan.

Relationship between ProsperScore and Borrower Rate¶

High Prosper score borrowers have the benefit of being granted low interest rate. The highest score is 11 which is the best(Low risk score) will attract a lower interest rate compared to 1 (High risk score).

Do Employment status affect loan application?¶

Employed people have a high chances of applying and getting loan.
